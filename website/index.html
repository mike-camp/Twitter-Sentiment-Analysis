Twitter analysis

In today's world, trends appear and disappear in a matter of days or even hours. Twitter is often at the forefront of this, providing a rich data source containing news announcements, advice, memes, and politics. By analyzing twitter data, your company can find out about the next trend before it blows up.

However, not all trends are created equal. If #RollTide because Alabama just won a game, then advertising based on this trend would be a good decision. However #RollTide could also be trending due to a major scandal in NCAA football, in which advertising based on this could be disastrous.

Location matters. Referencing #RollTide might be a good business decision in Alabama, but a horrible business decision in Louisiana. This website was created to address this challenge. By analyzing current tweets by not only sentiment, but also location, you can now adjust marketing strategies in real time.
